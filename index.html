Welcome to Twitter-flipflop.

Twitter flip flop is an automated program that uses tweepy and alteryx output when available in order to track politicians on Twitter and how their opinions flip flop. This helps people like us hold politicians accountable for their opinions and can show us how opinions change using politicians' own words! Check it out on Twitter
